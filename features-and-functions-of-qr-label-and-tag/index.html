Features and Functions of QR Label and Tag

For the Business:

  • Potentially combine all labels into one
  • Datalog customers when they scan the QR code
  • Advertising:
    • Advertise promotions/sales
    • Advertise a replacement when the customer needs to dispose of their item(SB707)
  • Security:
    • Serialization and tracking of each unique item/style
    • Check for authenticity

For the Customer:

  • Quality of Life:
    • Detect and display all content in the user's main language for their device
    • Easy access to a link to buy a replacement for their item
    • Communicate with washer/dryers that read QR codes via smartphone apps for care instructions
  • Care/Repair:
    • Access content and care instructions
      • Find regional or country laws for care
  • Repair:
    • Find out how/where to repair their item
    • Access customer service
  • History:
    • Seller
    • Previous owners
    • Returns
    • Repair events and reasons
  • Disposal:
    • Find out where to dispose of their item for SB707
  • Sustainability:
    • Find out where the materials were sourced from
    • Find out their item's carbon footprint
